About Sunshine Daycare

Dedicated to providing a warm, nurturing, and educational environment for the Châteauguay community since 2010.

Our Mission & Story

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate early childhood educators, Sunshine Daycare was built on a simple premise: children learn best when they are happy, safe, and encouraged to explore.

Our mission is to foster holistic development in young children through a balanced, play-based curriculum. We believe every child is unique and deserves a supportive space to explore, create, and build a strong foundation for lifelong learning. By integrating both English and French daily, we give children the cognitive advantages of bilingualism early in life.

  • Certified Early Childhood Educators
  • Nutritious meals and snacks provided daily
  • Large outdoor nature play area

Nutrition & Meals

Growing bodies need excellent fuel! We provide a nutritious, chef-prepared hot lunch and two healthy snacks (morning and afternoon) every single day. Our menus rotate over a 4-week cycle and strictly adhere to Canada's Food Guide.

We are a Nut-Free Facility. We accommodate allergies and dietary restrictions (vegetarian, halal, lactose-free) upon request. Mealtime is also an educational experience where children learn table manners, portion control, and how to serve themselves.

Tuition & Subsidies

Sunshine Daycare is a recognized childcare provider holding a permit from the Ministère de la Famille. We offer both standard rates and participate in the reduced contribution program.

  • Non-Subsidized Base Rate $45 / day
  • Subsidized Rate (CPE/Reduced) $9.10 / day

* Non-subsidized spaces are eligible for the Quebec childcare expense tax credit, which reimburses up to 78% of costs in monthly installments based on family income.

Frequently Asked Questions

We ask parents to provide indoor shoes, a seasonal change of clothes, a water bottle, and a comfort item (like a blanket) for nap time. For infants, parents provide diapers, wipes, and formula/breastmilk.

To protect all children, a child must be symptom-free (no fever, vomiting, or diarrhea) without medication for 24 hours before returning to daycare. We strictly adhere to Quebec public health guidelines.

We are closed on all provincial statutory holidays. We also close for a one-week winter break between Christmas and New Year's, and a one-week summer break in July. A calendar is provided upon enrollment.

We use a secure mobile app where educators post daily updates, photos, and notes about your child's meals, naps, and activities. You can also message the educators directly during business hours.
